PROCESS

The drawing and check-in process will occur in stages: rough, cleaning/coloring, and shading. Not all commissions require all stages.

The rough stage is defined as a sketchy rendering of your character(s).

The cleaning stage is defined as a clean-lined and clean-colored render of your character(s).

The shading stage is defined as a colored and shaded rendering of your character(s).

After each stage, I will send a work-in-progress (WIP) image to check in and make sure the piece is turning out how you want. I will make a maximum of 5 small changes after each stage. If the commissioner has approved a stage or if 5 small changes have been made to that stage, that aspect of the drawing will not be revised in future stages.

You may ask for updates and progress screenshots at any time, even outside of the automatic check-ins I perform.

Redlining (the act of drawing over an image for correction/demonstration purposes) my WIP images is encouraged. Providing any outside supplemental images is also encouraged to give me the best idea of what you are looking for.

WHAT I CAN AND CAN NOT DO

I will attempt most species. For bipedal non-human characters, I will draw the character's legs consistent with the reference unless otherwise requested (such as digitigrade, plantigrade, etc.) or I'll choose if neither are supplied.

I will not copy other people's or company's art styles outside of eyes occasionally or other specific circumstances.

I reserve the right to display my work anywhere and in any way at any stage of the process unless otherwise discussed.

I am an adult and I will take on not-safe-for-work (NSFW)/18+/explicit commissions from adults only. There will be an upcharge based on the severity of the content. A specific guideline for this is still in the works so ask me directly for a proper quote.

You may ask if I can do anything that is not covered in this TOS.

WHAT YOU CAN AND CAN NOT DO

You may edit work of mine that you own in any manner you see fit so long as the watermark or signature remains intact and completely visible.

If you did not buy it or if it does not otherwise belong to you, do not edit it!

Do not claim it to be your own work outside of any edits you make.

You may not use any of my work commercially unless terms have already been discussed and agreed upon.

You may re-post my work anywhere as long as proper credit is given. Linking back to the original post and/or artist somewhere in the description or on the image itself is encouraged but not necessary.
